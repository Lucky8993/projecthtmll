About Us

St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management (SAITM), Gurgaon, is a private technical and management institution established in 2012. Located in the industrial hub of Farrukhnagar, Gurgaon, the institute is spread across a 22-acre green campus and is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak.
Academic Profile & Accreditations Approvals: Approved by the AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). Accreditations: Accredited with an 'A+' Grade by NAAC and holds NBA accreditation for its Computer Science Engineering program. Rankings: Ranked 8th among top engineering colleges by The Times of India. Ranked 28th among the best academic engineering colleges by India Today. Classified as a Tier 2 institute due to moderate placement scales and absence from top NIRF rankings. Placements & Internships SAITM operates a dedicated Training & Placement (T&P) cell that maintains ties with over 70 recruiters.
